50 Years Of Experience


The consultancy has dedicated Fire Protection Engineers, and has more than 50 years of combined experience to draw on. The company is well versed in the knowledge and expertise to interpret relevant rules, regulations and by-laws in the mass of sometimes confusing information, regarding installation codes of practice and other ambiguous regulations laid down by various Authorities. Our portfolio of work extends into Africa, there are numerous buildings within Ghana which the practice has been involved in and there are plans for future buildings as well. These buildings have been commercial office spaces, and in many instances have included for the protection of IT infrastructure. Other countries where we have had involvement are Nigeria and Zambia. Locally, our practice has been involved in the ATNS OR Tambo Airport and ATNS Cape Town..
